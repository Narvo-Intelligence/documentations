Narvo Component Design System

A modular, editorial, audio-first component library for the full product.

The inspiration is the floating card language, soft modularity, and clarity of grouping. Narvo pushes it further: calmer reading surfaces, stronger proof-of-trust modules, and a more distinctive audio layer built on Petrol Teal, Signal Vermilion, Newsreader, and Instrument Sans.

Signature Families: Story + Truth + Player Primary UI Tone: Calm, Modular, Precise Audio Surfaces Carry More Energy Cards Are Rounded but Not Soft-Generic

Skeleton

System Read

The three signature families are Story, Truth, and Player.

Everything else should support those three families. That is how Narvo avoids becoming a generic content app with isolated good-looking pieces.

Rules

Reading surfaces stay quieter than audio surfaces.
Signal Vermilion is action and urgency, not the base UI colour.
Truth modules must feel precise, mono-led, and Narvo-specific.
Rounded cards are modular and premium, not playful or generic.

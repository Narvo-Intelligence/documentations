Visual Conclusion

The Narvo system works best as a calm intelligence product with controlled bursts of urgency.

The visual engine is Petrol Teal across dashboard structure, reading surfaces, voice tools, and the player shell. Signal Vermilion stays reserved for breaking states, live moments, and urgent actions. That balance lets the storytelling layer stay premium while the modern, Gen‑Z energy appears exactly where it matters.

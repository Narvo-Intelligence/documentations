Approval Logic

Why this should be the final direction

The product needs long-session visual endurance, clear audio controls, and confidence signals. Petrol Teal supports all three. Vermilion stays present, but only where urgency, live moments, and breaking states need a visible spike.

Approval Recommendation

Approve the Petrol Teal system as Narvo’s core design language.

The final rule set is straightforward: Teal owns identity, structure, and everyday use. Vermilion owns urgency and live attention. Editorial typography gives the product a point of view, while cool surfaces and strict truth-state semantics keep the experience usable and credible over long sessions.
